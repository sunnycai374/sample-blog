About Shanghai

Shanghai is a vibrant blend of modern skyscrapers and traditional culture. From the iconic skyline of Pudong to the historic charm of the Bund, this city offers an unforgettable mix of east and west.

Top Places to Visit

The Bund

Walk along the Huangpu River and enjoy Shanghai’s skyline at night.

Yu Garden

A classical Chinese garden filled with bridges, koi ponds, and history.

Shanghai Tower

Experience breathtaking views from one of the tallest buildings in the world.

Must-Try Food

Xiaolongbao (Soup Dumplings)

Shanghai’s most famous dish—delicate dumplings filled with hot soup.

Shengjianbao

Crispy pan-fried buns with juicy pork filling.

Scallion Oil Noodles

A simple yet flavorful local favorite.

Travel Tips

  • Use metro – fast and cheap 🚇
  • Carry cash + digital payment apps
  • Visit in spring or autumn for best weather
  • Download maps offline (Google Maps may be limited)
